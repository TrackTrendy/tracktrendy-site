The problem

Analytics tools got complicated. And risky.

Google Analytics 4 is powerful - but in the EU, using it without proper consent is a legal grey area. Cookie banners damage conversions. And most alternatives are either too simple or too expensive.

There should be a better way to understand your website traffic without putting your visitors' privacy at risk.

  • GA4 requires cookie consent in the EU - and most banners reduce conversions by up to 10%
  • GA4's interface is complex - most users can't find the data they actually need
  • Your visitor data is sent to Google's servers and used to build advertising profiles
  • Privacy-first alternatives like Plausible lack revenue tracking, goals, and GSC integration
  • No analytics tool explains what your numbers actually mean - you need a data analyst

FAQ

Questions we get asked a lot

What is TrackTrendy?

TrackTrendy is a privacy-first website analytics platform. It gives you detailed insight into your website traffic - visitors, sources, pages, countries, goals and revenue - without using cookies or tracking your visitors across the web. It's designed as a GDPR-compliant alternative to Google Analytics 4.

Is TrackTrendy GDPR compliant?

Yes, by design. TrackTrendy does not use cookies or persistent identifiers, does not collect personal data, and processes all data in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR. Your visitor data is never sent to third parties or used for advertising purposes.

Do I need a cookie consent banner with TrackTrendy?

No. Because TrackTrendy does not use cookies or collect personal data, you do not need a cookie consent banner for analytics. This improves your conversion rate by removing a friction point from your visitors' experience.

How is TrackTrendy different from Google Analytics?

The key differences: TrackTrendy is cookie-free (no consent banners needed), your data stays private and is never shared with or used by third parties, and the interface is designed to be immediately readable without training. GA4 requires cookie consent in the EU, sends data to Google's servers, and has a steep learning curve.

How does TrackTrendy compare to Plausible?

Both are privacy-first and cookie-free. TrackTrendy adds revenue tracking (WooCommerce), a built-in UTM campaign builder, traffic spike email alerts, team roles on all plans, and an AI assistant (coming soon) - all from €4/month. Plausible starts at $9/month and reserves some features for higher tiers.

How do I add TrackTrendy to my website?

Add one line of JavaScript to your site's <head> section. TrackTrendy works with any platform - WordPress, WooCommerce, Shopify, Webflow, or custom HTML. Your tracking snippet is shown in your dashboard after signing up, and is live in under a minute.

Does TrackTrendy work with WooCommerce?

Yes. TrackTrendy has built-in WooCommerce revenue tracking. Add a short PHP snippet to your theme (or via Code Snippets) and purchase events, order values, and total revenue appear in your dashboard - broken down by traffic source, campaign, and channel.

Is there a free trial?

Yes - every new account gets 30 days free with full access to all features. No credit card required to start. After the trial, the Single Site plan is €4/month and the Multi-Site plan (up to 15 sites) is €15/month.
